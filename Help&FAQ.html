Help & FAQ

• What is the minimum age to attend music festivals?

The minimum age limit to enter a festival is 18. This is a strict policy and anybody born in 2003 or later, even if accompanied by an adult, will not be able to enter a festival.

• Can I enter anytime?

As long as you are holding a ticket that has not been scanned that day, you may enter any time during the event. Ins and outs are not allowed.

• How can I avoid ticket fraud?

The best way to avoid ticket fraud is to buy directly from ticketing partner for that show. If you arrive at the festival with an illegitimate ticket, you will be turned away, and you will not get a refund.

• I can no longer attend a festival. Can I have a refund?

As stated in the terms of purchase, you will not get refunds or exchanges. If you can no longer attend the festival, we recommend you gift or sell your ticket to a friend or family member.

• Can I bring my prescribed medication into the events?

For specific information or any further questions regarding prescribed medication please contact us for detailed on how to bring your medication into our shows.

• What forms of ID are acceptable?

Passport or ID card.

• Will I be able to buy food and drinks at festivals?

A wide variety of food and drinks will be available all day and night at festivals. PLEASE NOTE: No food or drinks will be allowed to be brought into the festival.

Tips & Tricks

• Do not wear your best and favorite clothes. It is very possible that your clothes will get dirty or the weather will be bad. You can also take spare clothes with you.

• Do not wear your favorite shoes. There are always a lot of people at festivals and will constantly step on your feet. Also, bad weather will obviously ruin your shoes. Better take spare shoes with you.

• Find the festival map and artist schedule. Festivals are always quite extensive, so it’s better to have a festival card with you so that you don’t get lost in the crowd. Also take the schedule of performances so as not to miss the performance of your favorite DJ.

• Get a strap for your camera or phone. So that your phone and camera do not get lost or fall or break, take your strap with you. There are also many pickpockets at festivals, so it's best to keep the most important things with you.

• Bring different bank cards with you. If you lose one of your bank cards, it is better to have a spare with you. Also don’t forget put your bank cards in different places.

• Take a money belt. So as not to lose your money purchase a money belt. With it, all the necessary things will be with you and it will be impossible to steal them.

• Pack hand sanitizer and a toilet paper. To maintain personal hygiene, remember to use the hand sanitizer before you eat or after using a toilet. Also take toilet paper with you, as in toilets it runs out very quickly and usually the staff does not monitor the availability of toilet paper.

• Take a portable charger with you. To be able to capture all the unforgettable moments of the festival on your phone or camera, do not forget to bring a portable charger with you.
